Booking Policy

Booking process, payment terms, and service confirmation rules

Welcome to Web Tours and Travels. This Booking Policy outlines the terms related to reservations, payments, and safety standards.


  • 1 • Booking Process
    • Bookings can be made via call, WhatsApp, or online platforms.
    • Customers must provide accurate details including pickup location, drop location, date, time, and trip type.
    • Booking is considered confirmed only after advance payment (if applicable).

  • 2 • Advance Payment
    • Local city rides: Advance may or may not be required.
    • Outstation trips: Minimum advance payment is mandatory.
    • The advance amount depends on trip type, distance, and vehicle category.

  • 3 • Payment Terms
    • Accepted payment methods: UPI, Bank Transfer, and Cash.
    • Remaining balance must be paid:
      • Before trip start, OR
      • During the trip (as agreed)
    • Failure to complete payment may result in:
      • Trip cancellation
      • Extra waiting charges
      • Legal recovery action (in extreme cases)

  • 4 • Pricing Policy
    • Charges are based on distance, duration, and vehicle type.
    • Additional charges may include:
      • Toll tax
      • Parking charges
      • State tax
      • Driver allowance (for outstation)
    • All applicable charges are communicated in advance.

  • 5 • Cancellation & No-Show
    • Cancellation 12 hours before trip: Eligible for refund (as per policy).
    • Cancellation before 6 hours of trip: Partial or no refund.
    • No-show (customer not available): No refund.
    • Booking must be placed 24 hours before trip.

  • 6 • Driver & Vehicle Safety
    • All drivers are verified and trained professionals.
    • Drivers strictly follow traffic rules and safety protocols.
    • Vehicles are regularly serviced and inspected before trips.

  • 7 • Passenger Safety Responsibilities
    • Seatbelt usage is mandatory.
    • Customers must not:
      • Distract the driver
      • Use abusive language
      • Engage in unsafe behavior
    • In case of misconduct, the driver has the right to terminate the trip.

  • 8 • Luggage & Belongings
    • Customers must inform in advance about heavy or extra luggage.
    • The company is not responsible for:
      • Lost items
      • Forgotten belongings

  • 9 • Delays & Uncontrollable Situations
    • Delays due to traffic, weather, road conditions, or government restrictions are not under company control.
    • No liability for missed flights, trains, or appointments.

  • 10 • Emergency & Support
    • In case of breakdown or emergency:
      • Replacement vehicle will be arranged where possible.
      • Support team will assist immediately.

  • 11 • Right to Refuse Service
    • The company reserves the right to refuse or cancel booking in case of:
      • Safety concerns
      • Misconduct
      • Violation of policy

  • 12 • Booking Modifications
    • Changes to date, time, or route must be requested at least 6 hours before the trip.
    • Vehicle upgrades are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
    • Route changes during the trip may affect the final fare.

By booking with Web Tours and Travels, you agree to all the above terms.
