Privacy Policy

Data collection, usage, storage, and customer privacy rights

Web Tours and Travels is committed to protecting customer privacy and handling personal information responsibly.


  • 1 • Information We Collect
    We may collect the following information during booking or interaction:
    • Name
    • Phone number
    • Pickup and drop location
    • Travel details (date, time, trip type)
    • Payment details (limited to transaction reference)

  • 2 • Purpose of Data Collection
    Customer information is collected only for:
    • Booking confirmation and trip execution
    • Communication regarding service updates
    • Customer support and issue resolution
    • Internal record keeping

  • 3 • Data Sharing Policy
    • Customer data may be shared with:
      • Assigned drivers
      • Operational partners (if required for trip execution)
    • We do not sell, rent, or trade customer data to third parties.

  • 4 • Data Security
    • Reasonable measures are taken to protect customer data from unauthorized access.
    • However, no system is completely secure, and customers share information at their own risk.

  • 5 • Communication Policy
    • Customers may receive:
      • Booking confirmations
      • Trip updates
      • Service-related communication
    • Promotional communication will be limited and optional where applicable.

  • 6 • Data Retention
    • Customer data may be stored for operational and legal purposes.
    • Data is retained only as long as necessary.

  • 7 • Customer Responsibility
    • Customers must provide accurate information.
    • The company is not responsible for issues caused by incorrect details.

  • 8 • Third-Party Services
    • If payments or bookings are made through third-party platforms, their privacy policies may also apply.

  • 9 • Policy Updates
    • Web Tours and Travels reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice.

  • 10 • Consent
    • By using our services, customers agree to the collection and use of their information as outlined in this policy.

  • 11 • How We Use Your Data
    • To process and confirm your bookings.
    • To communicate trip updates and offers.
    • To improve our services and user experience.

  • 12 • Data Protection
    • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
    • Data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.
