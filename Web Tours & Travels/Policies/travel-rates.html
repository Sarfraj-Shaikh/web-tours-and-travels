Travel Rates Policy

Pricing structure, vehicle rates, extra charges, and applicable conditions

At Web Tours and Travels, we follow a transparent and structured pricing system to ensure clarity and fairness for all customers.


  • 1 • Rate Structure
    • Travel charges are calculated based on:
    • Distance (per kilometer)
    • Duration (per day/hour)
    • Vehicle type selected

  • 2 • Minimum Billing
    • A minimum daily kilometer limit applies (e.g., 250 km/day for outstation trips).
    • Even if usage is lower, minimum charges will be applicable.

  • 3 • Inclusions
    Base fare typically includes:
    • Vehicle usage
    • Driver service
    • Fuel charges

  • 4 • Exclusions (Extra Charges)
    The following are not included in base fare unless specified:
    • Toll charges
    • Parking fees
    • State entry taxes
    • Driver allowance (for outstation/multi-day trips)

  • 5 • Night Charges
    • Additional charges may apply for travel between 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM.

  • 6 • Waiting Charges
    • Waiting time beyond agreed duration may be charged on hourly basis.

  • 7 • Outstation Travel
    • Charges are calculated on:
    • Round-trip distance basis (garage to garage)
    • Minimum daily kilometers
    • Driver allowance is charged per day.

  • 8 • One-Way Trips
    • One-way bookings may include return distance charges unless otherwise agreed.

  • 9 • Airport Transfers
    • Fixed rates apply within city limits.
    • Waiting beyond free time will incur extra charges.

  • 10 • Peak Pricing
    • Rates may increase during:
    • Festivals
    • Weekends
    • High-demand periods

  • 11 • Payment Terms
    • Advance payment may be required to confirm booking.
    • Full payment must be completed before trip or after trip completion.

  • 12 • Pricing Transparency
    • All charges will be communicated before booking confirmation.
    • No hidden charges will be applied beyond agreed terms.

  • 13 • Changes in Trip
    • Any route change, extra distance, or extended usage will result in additional charges.

  • 14 • Disputes
    • Any pricing disputes must be raised during or immediately after the trip.
    • Final billing will be based on agreed terms and actual usage.

We are committed to providing reliable, fair, and transparent pricing for every journey.


* Rates are subject to change. Final pricing provided at time of booking confirmation.
